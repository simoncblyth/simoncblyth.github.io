GGeoTest¶

Creates simple test geometries from a commandline specification.

Canonical instance m_geotest resides in OpticksHub and is instanciated only when the –test geometry option is active. This happens after standard geometry is loaded via OpticksHub::modifyGeometry.

Rejig¶

  • GGeoTest is now a GGeoBase subclass (just like GGeo and GScene)
  • GGeoTest now has its own GGeoLib, to avoid dirty modifyGeometry appropach which cleared the basis mm

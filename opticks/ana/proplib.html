PropLib provides access to optical properties of materials and surfaces as a function of wavelength.

Material properties:

REFRACTIVE_INDEX
ABSORPTION_LENGTH
SCATTERING_LENGTH
REEMISSION_PROB

Surface properties:

DETECT
ABSORB
REFLECT_SPECULAR
REFLECT_DIFFUSE

Example data shapes:

Out[5]: (38, 2, 39, 4)

In [6]: slib.data.shape
Out[6]: (48, 2, 39, 4)

In [7]: blib.data.shape
Out[7]: (123, 4, 2, 39, 4)

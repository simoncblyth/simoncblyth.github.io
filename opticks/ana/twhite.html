twhite.py: Wavelength Distribution Check¶

Creates plot comparing simulated photon wavelength spectrum from twhite- : Pencil beam of white light incident on Glass prism against blackbody expectation.

This is checking the source_lookup implementation and the inverse CDF source_texture that it uses.

treflect.py¶

See treflect- : Fresnel reflection vs incident angle check

Loads S and P polarized events, selects photons that have histories : “TO BR SA” or “TO BR AB”, ie photons that reflect at the first interface and are either bulk absorbed (AB) or surface absorbed (SA).

A ratio of histograms of the angle of incidence (theta) for all photons with reflecting photons is compared against expectations of the Fresnel formula.

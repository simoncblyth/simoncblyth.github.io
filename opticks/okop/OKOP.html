Thoughts¶

To some extent the OpticksHub is getting everywhere just as having a shared context is convenient : rather than for its functionality. Where that is the case probably better to replace with an OpticksCtx that just acts to hold on to things.

OpKernel in planning¶

Aiming for the simplest possible way to take a set of gensteps, pass to GPU, generate and propagate photons and copy back hits. No frills, except perhaps indexing

OpticksEvent* m_event ;
OpPropagator* m_propagator ;

Review these : see if they are doing anything not needed.

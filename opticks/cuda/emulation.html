Portland Group PGI x86 CUDA Compiler¶

Commercial compilers

PGI CUDA-x86 support is included in PGI’s GPU-enabled C/C++ compiler products (the PGI Accelerator products). PGI Accelerator licensees with a current PGI Subscription get PGI CUDA-x86 at no extra charge. Customers without GPU-enabled licenses can upgrade by paying just the difference in the license fee for a GPU-enabled version. Contact PGI sales for more information.

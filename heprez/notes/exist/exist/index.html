SCHEDULE HEPREZ UPDATE WITH CRON¶

Uncomment and schedule the time to start:

b2mc:cron heprez$ crontab -l
SHELL=/bin/bash
PATH=/usr/bin:/bin:/usr/sbin:/sbin:/usr/local/bin:/usr/X11/bin:/opt/local/bin
NODE_TAG_OVERRIDE=K
HOME=/Users/heprez
ENV_HOME=/Users/heprez/env
HEPREZ_HOME=/Users/heprez/heprez
#
15 18 * * * (. /Users/heprez/env/env.bash ; env- ; . /Users/heprez/heprez/heprez.bash ; heprez-  ; heprez-update ) > $HOME/cron/heprez-update-$(date +"\%Y\%m\%d-\%H\%M").log  2>&1

Follow the scrape run, summary view:

b2mc:logs heprez$ cd ~/cron
b2mc:cron heprez$ tail -f  heprez-update-20130703-1815.log

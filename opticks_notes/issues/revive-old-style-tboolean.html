revive old style tboolean funs¶

Overview¶

Old style tboolean-bib- functions are still a generation ahead of the integration tests in geometry config. So as a stepping stone to getting integration tests operational revive the three remaining old style tboolean funcs.

simon:ggeo blyth$ tboolean-ls | grep bib

tboolean-bib-box
tboolean-bib-box-small-offset-sphere
tboolean-bib-box-sphere
